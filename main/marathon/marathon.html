Return Trip

The Experience

Marathon runners are a bit like aviators if you really think about it. Each runner spends an incredible amount of time training, dieting, focusing, researching, and mentally preparing for what inevitably could end up being a "bonk" or in pilot lingo ... "A crash and burn".

Over the past two to two and a half years, I have been interviewing and compiling marathon runners data for two specific racers. Each about the same age; however, they are the opposite gender. At the beginning of this experience, my hypothesis was that female runners could withstand higher training mileage that would produce better variant times year over year than male runners.

My Role

Engage in the running community by discussing different training techniques, habits, diets, thresholds, and injury reports to ultimately compare the differences between "Racer F" and "Racer M".
